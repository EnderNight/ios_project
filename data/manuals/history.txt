
--NAME--
    history - display a list of the most recently executed commands

--USAGE--
    history

--OPTIONS--
    None

--DESCRIPTION--
    The history command displays a list of the most recently executed commands in the shell.
    The number of commands displayed is limited to 10 by default, but can be increased by setting the NUM_CMD environment variable to a larger value.

--ARGUMENTS--
    None

--SYSTEM CALLS--
    -open
    -read
    -close
    -write
